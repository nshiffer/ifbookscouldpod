About the Podcast

Deconstructing the bestsellers that shaped popular culture

Mission

We critique popular nonfiction "airport books" — those bestsellers that promise simple answers to complex problems. Through humor and careful analysis, we examine their flawed arguments, poor data usage, and unsound conclusions.

Monthly Episodes

Since 2022, we've been releasing monthly deep-dives into the books that have shaped popular discourse. Each episode runs approximately 60 minutes and focuses on a single influential title.
